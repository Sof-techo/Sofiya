Spicy Heaven Ethiopian Restaurant

Sofiya Mohammed

Description

This website serves as promotional media for the Ethiopian restaurant located in the heart of the city of Leuven. The restaurant has been in business for the last five years, but it has not increased revenue in the last three years. It needs to attract new customers to increase its market share. With most target customers being tourists, the website needs to be user-friendly and appealing to viewers.

Target Customers

Target customers are mainly tourists visiting Leuven, both locals and international tourists, as well as local residents.

Purpose

The goal of the website is to increase the visibility of the restaurant and attract more customers to boost its revenue.

Content

I will create four pages:

  • Home: Brief information about the restaurant, origin of the food, opening hours, logo, and navigation buttons to other pages.
  • About Us: Detailed information about the restaurant and the dishes served.
  • Our Menu: A list of available dishes, brief explanations, illustrative pictures, and respective prices.
  • Contact Us: A page to interact with customers, including the full address of the restaurant and a contact form.
